## Multimodal Semantics
My dissertation research develops formal semantic representations for non-verbal communication in dialogue. This includes **GAMR** (Gesture Abstract Meaning Representation), an extension of AMR for capturing gesture meaning, and work on **grunts** as complete communicative acts with distinct pragmatic functions.

**Resources:** [Annotation Guidelines (PDF)](/files/Grunts_Annotation_Conventions.pdf)

**Key publications:** [Abstract meaning representation for gesture](/publication/2022-gamr


## Culinary Linguistics
I apply computational methods to analyze food discourse, examining how language constructs authenticity and cultural identity. Projects include **R2VQ** (Recipe-to-Video Question Answering) and **Just Like My Nonna Made It:** Authority and Authenticity in Italian-American Tomato Sauce Recipes (forthcoming chapter on sociolinguistics and identity formation in Italian-American recipes).

**Resources:** [R2VQ](https://competitions.codalab.org/competitions/34056)


## Collaborative Learning Technology
As a student researcher with **[iSAT](https://www.colorado.edu/research/ai-institute/)** (NSF Institute for Student-AI Teaming), I have worked on frameworks for understanding human collaboration in educational contexts. This includes work on common ground tracking and situated dialogue in collaborative problem-solving tasks.

I have also published on Digital Literacy in elementary CS education.
